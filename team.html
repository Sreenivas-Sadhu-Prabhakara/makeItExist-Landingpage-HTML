Meet the Team Behind the Magic

The Weekend
Sprint.

From whiteboard concept on Friday to deployed application on Sunday. We're a group of AIM students who believe great ideas deserve to exist — and we make sure they do.

The 48-Hour Process

Every project follows our battle-tested weekend sprint framework. Fast, focused, and fully deployed.

1
FRIDAY PM

The Concept

Ideation and roadmapping in the study lounge. We define scope, pick tools, and sketch wireframes.

2
SATURDAY AM/PM

The Build

Heavy lifting — coding, designing, and caffeine-fueled sprints in the computer lab.

3
SUNDAY EVE

The Launch

QA, deployment to Vercel, and handover. Ready for the student body by Sunday night.

What Drives Us

We're not a company. We're a student collective with a shared mission: make tech accessible for every AIM student.

Zero Profit

Every project is done at cost or free. We don't charge labor — only pass-through hosting fees when needed.

Speed First

Our weekend sprint model means ideas don't sit in backlogs. We ship fast and iterate faster.

Learn by Doing

Every project is a learning opportunity. We grow our skills while delivering real value to the community.

Community

Open to all AIM students regardless of program. Designers, strategists, and coders — everyone has a role.
